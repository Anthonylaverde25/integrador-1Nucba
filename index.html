Overview Add to Cart

T he peaceful life of the inhabitants of Isla de las Sombras is shaken when a group of young people decide to delve into the dark mysteries that surround the legendary abandoned lighthouse known as "La Cuna de los Ahogados". Marina, a brave teenager with an enigmatic past, leads her friends on this intrepid quest. Together, they venture into the labyrinthine underground tunnels that connect the lighthouse to the village, plunging into a world of terrifying shadows and sinister whispers that seem to rise from the very depths of the abyss. As they unravel the secrets hidden in the island's past, they come across a series of unexplained disappearances that have haunted Isle of Shadows for generations. Each discovery brings them closer to a disturbing truth, as danger closes in on them and time runs against them. Book Details ...
